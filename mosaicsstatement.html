Mosaics Statement

In 2008 I was invited to do three mosaic murals in three different schools in Queens NYC. I developed a process and taught the students to collectively design and fabricate their own mosaic. The fourth mosaic I was asked to do was my own work, but I still included student built mosaics on a pair of pillars outside the lobby.
